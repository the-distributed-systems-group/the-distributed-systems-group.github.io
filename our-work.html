Our work

The DSG are working towards our vision and strategy by way of a number of programmes. The component parts of these programmes have been carefully co-designed across government, industry and academia and may be composed to solve different complex problems.

The Utility Trade Platform

A distributed, highly tamper resistant, assured system of record co-designed and built by gov, industry and academia.

Visit the UTP programme site (under construction) for more information.

The Open Ecosystem Federation

An ecosystem/network toolkit enabling safe, easy to understand sharing of expert opinions and intelligence.

Visit the OEF programme site for more information.
